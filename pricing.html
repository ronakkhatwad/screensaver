What is Covered in Our Annual Manintainance Plan?

New and Old Phones

We provide Annual Maintainance Plans for both new and old mobile phones.

Accidental Screen Damage

Our Annual Maintainance Plan provides complete screen replacement and service against accidental damage of mobile display.

One Year

Your mobile display will be covered under our annual maintainance plan for a period of One Year. We will replace your mobile's damaged screen only for One Time under an active Maintainance Plan.

How to Claim Service

You can claim service for your damaged mobile screen by following this very easy steps.

  1. STEP 1 - Visit your nearest OneSecure Service Center or call us at +91 90-991-991-51.
  2. STEP 2 - We will completely inspect your mobile to determine damages to your screen.
  3. STEP 3 - After service approval, we will repair your mobile screen within 7 days.
  4. STEP 4 - Once repair is done, you can collect your mobile from service center.

What is not covered in our Annual Maintainance Plan?

Liquid Damage

we do not cover any screen damages due to liquids in our maintainance plans.



Electronic and Mechanical Damages

We do not cover any mechanical or electronic damage in our maintainance plans.

Accessories Damage

We do not provide any repair service for damaged accessories of mobile in our maintainance plans.

Pre-Existing Damage

We will not provide maintainance plans for those mobile if mobile screen is already damanged.
