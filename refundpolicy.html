Refund and Cancellation Policy

  1. After the payment of the Plan Fee (all inclusive) and/or Activation of Annual Maintenance Plan(s) by OneSecure, the Annual Maintenance Plan(s) cannot be terminated and no refund will be available.
  2. If the Annual Maintenance Plan(s) is Rejected due to providing incorrect IMEI Number or incorrect mobile company or incorrect mobile model or incorrect mobile images or mobile display is damaged already, Plan Fee after deducting the transaction charges shall be refunded.
  3. If OneSecure fails to activate Annual Maintenance Plan(s) purchased by customer within provided time period, complete Plan Fee will be refunded to customer.
  4. If the Customer has lodged a claim or availed of any benefit under any of the Plan Terms any time during the term of the plan, no refund will be available.

  • 2% of Amount payable for normal debit and credit cards, netbanking, upi, wallets.
